Coldplay



Coldplay is a British rock band. Formed in London in 1996 by vocalist and pianist Chris Martin and guitarist John Buckland, later joined by bassist Guy Berryman and drummer Will Champion. The band's style of music, vocals and composition contain many characteristics associated with alternative rock and post-Britpop
