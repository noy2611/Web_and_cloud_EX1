Maroon 5



Maroon 5 is a pop rock band from Los Angeles, winner of three Grammy Awards, which combines elements of funk and soul music in its songs. The band's breakthrough came following the hit "This Love" from 2004

Comments

  • The band's debut album, "Songs About Jane". The first single from the album was "Harder to Breathe", which entered the TOP 20 in the United States and the United Kingdom.
  • Three members of the band have known each other since middle school
